# 🌿 Garden Spirit

> Water your garden, unlock flowers, and watch your spirit guide evolve!

---

## 🧚 About

Garden Spirit is a browser-based idle clicker game with a cozy garden theme. Click to water your plant, earn growth points, and unlock new flowers as your garden blooms. Upgrade your watering power to grow faster — and watch your spirit guide transform as the garden flourishes!

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. Click **💧 Water Plant** to earn growth points
2. Each click adds **1 growth point** (or more after upgrading)
3. Unlock flowers as your growth increases
4. Spend **10 growth points** on **⬆ Upgrade Watering** to increase your watering power
5. Keep clicking to reach higher growth milestones and transform your spirit guide!

---

## 🌸 Flower Unlocks

| Growth Required | Flower Unlocked |
|----------------|-----------------|
| 5 | 🌼 Daisy |
| 15 | 🌷 Tulip |
| 30 | 🌸 Cherry Blossom |
| 50 | 🌺 Hibiscus |

Flowers stay unlocked permanently — they don't reset when you upgrade.

## ⚙️ Game Details

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Starting growth | 0 |
| Starting watering power | 1 pt per click |
| Upgrade cost | 10 growth points |
| Power increase per upgrade | +1 pt per click |
| Character animation | Scale bounce on each water click |

---

## 🖥️ How to Run

1. Place all files in the same folder
2. Open `index.html` in any modern browser
3. No installation or internet connection required

---

## 💡 Tips

- Upgrade early and often — each upgrade permanently increases clicks per water
- Flowers unlock based on total growth, not current growth, so upgrading doesn't block unlocks
- Reach growth 50 to unlock all 4 flowers and the Fox spirit guide
- There's no end state — keep clicking to grow your garden as big as you like!
