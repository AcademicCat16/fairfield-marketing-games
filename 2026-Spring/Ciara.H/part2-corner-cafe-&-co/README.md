# ☕ Corner Café & Co.
### *A Cozy Life Simulation Game*

> Where every cup tells a story.

---

## 🌸 About

Corner Café & Co. is a relaxing browser-based life simulation game where you run your own cozy café. Serve customers their favorite drinks, grow your reputation, build a following on social media, and decorate your space — all at your own pace. There's no losing condition, just vibes.

---

## 🎮 How to Play

### Getting Started
Open `index.html` in any modern browser. No installation or dependencies needed.

### Gameplay Loop

1. **Wait for a customer** to walk in — they'll appear in the *Current Customer* card showing their name, mood, and favorite drink
2. **Select the matching drink** from the four buttons below the customer card
3. **Earn money and reputation** for every correct order
4. **Spend money** on new café décor to boost the atmosphere
5. **Post on social media** to grow your follower count
6. **Repeat** and watch your cozy café thrive ✨

## ✨ Features

- **Customer system** — randomly generated customers with names, moods, and drink preferences
- **Mood bar** — customers get unhappy if served the wrong drink; leave if mood hits zero
- **Décor unlocks** — spend $50 to unlock random café decorations (6 total)
- **Social media posting** — gain followers with randomly generated aesthetic captions
- **🌧️ Rain mode** — toggle a cozy rainy day overlay for extra atmosphere
- **Particle effects** — soft floating sparkles in the background
- **Celebration animations** — emoji burst on every successful order
- **Music indicator** — click the 🎵 button to imagine lo-fi café vibes

## 💡 Tips

- Unlocking décor gives a small bonus to every drink you serve — prioritize it early
- Posting on social is most effective after building up reputation first
- Wrong drink guesses cost customer mood — if unsure, read their *Loves:* hint carefully
- Customers spawn every 4–8 seconds automatically, so there's no rush
