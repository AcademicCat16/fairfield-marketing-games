❄️ Winter Horse Quest ❄️

How To Play

You are a brave winter steed battling through enchanted lands. Defeat each boss to progress!

  • Attack: Reliable damage every turn.
  • Heavy Attack: Big damage, but may miss.
  • Defend: Reduces next enemy attack.
  • Heal: Restore health (3 uses per boss).

Survive all bosses to win the game!
