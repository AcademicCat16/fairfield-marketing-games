# 🎓 Campus Hustle

> Click to grind, buy upgrades, earn passive income, and reach $10,000!

---

## 💰 About

Campus Hustle is a browser-based idle clicker game with a college money grind theme. Click the Grind button to earn cash, spend it on upgrades that boost your click value and generate passive income, and race to $10,000 to win!

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. Click **Grind** to earn money per click
2. Buy upgrades from the shop to earn more per click or passively per second
3. Reach **$10,000** to win!

---

## 🛒 Upgrades

| Upgrade | Cost | Effect |
|---------|------|--------|
| ☕ Coffee Boost | $50 | +1 per click (stacks — buy multiple times!) |
| 💼 Internship | $100 | +$1 per second passive income |
| 💡 Side Hustle | $200 | +$2 per second passive income |

All upgrades stack — buy them multiple times to keep increasing your income.

---

## ⚙️ Game Details

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Starting money | $0 |
| Starting click value | $1 |
| Starting passive income | $0/sec |
| Win condition | Reach $10,000 |
| Passive income tick | Every 1 second |

---

## 🖥️ How to Run

1. Place all files in the same folder
2. Open `index.html` in any modern browser
3. No installation or internet connection required

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **HTML** — layout, money display, shop buttons
- **CSS** — dark navy background, blue button styling
- **Vanilla JavaScript** — click handler, upgrade logic, setInterval passive income loop

---

## 💡 Tips

- Coffee Boost is the cheapest upgrade and stacks — spam it early to speed up earning
- One Internship ($100) pays for itself in 100 seconds — buy it as soon as you can
- Side Hustle ($200) gives double the passive rate of Internship — best long-term value
- Stack multiple Side Hustles for fast passive income and an easy path to $10,000
