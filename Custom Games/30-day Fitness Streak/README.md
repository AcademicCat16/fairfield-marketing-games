# 💪 30-Day Fitness Streak

> Click after every workout to build your streak and level up!

---

## 🏋️ About

30-Day Fitness Streak is a simple browser-based habit tracker. Click the button once after each workout session to grow your streak and unlock new fitness levels. It's a motivational tool to help build consistent exercise habits over 30 days.

---

## 🕹️ How to Use

1. Open the page
2. Complete a workout
3. Click **Complete Workout** to log it
4. Watch your streak grow and your level increase!

---

## 🏅 Levels

| Streak | Level |
|--------|-------|
| 0–9 workouts | Beginner |
| 10–19 workouts | Dedicated |
| 20+ workouts | Elite Athlete |

---

## ⚙️ Details

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Goal | 30 workouts |
| Starting level | Beginner |
| Streak tracking | Session only (resets on page refresh) |
| Button | One click = one workout logged |

Note: the streak is stored in memory only — refreshing the page resets it to 0.

---

- **HTML/CSS** — dark background, centred layout, green button
- **Vanilla JavaScript** — streak counter, level progression logic

---

## 💡 Tips

- Click once per day after your workout to keep the spirit of the 30-day challenge
- Reach 10 workouts to hit **Dedicated** — that's a third of the way there!
- Hit 20 to unlock **Elite Athlete** — the final level
- To restart your streak, simply refresh the page
