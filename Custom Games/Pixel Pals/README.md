# 🎮 Pixel Pals

A top-down pixel art game with an animated title screen. Collect pixel pals scattered around a cozy map before the timer runs out!

---

## How to Set Up in CodePen

1. Paste **HTML** into the **HTML** panel
2. Paste **CSS** into the **CSS** panel
3. Paste **JS** into the **JS** panel
4. No external libraries needed — the Press Start 2P font loads from Google Fonts automatically!

> ⚙️ If you see a null error, go to the JS panel gear icon → **"No wrap - bottom of body"**

---

## How to Play

1. Click **"Start a New Game"** on the title screen
2. Move your pixel character around the map using **WASD** or **Arrow Keys**
3. Walk into **colored pixel pals** to collect them and earn points
4. Collect as many as you can before the **60-second timer** runs out!
5. Click **"Return to Main Menu"** to go back anytime

---

## Scoring

| Pixel Pal | Shape | Points |
|---|---|---|
| 🟥 Pink square | Square | 1 pt |
| 🟡 Gold star | Star | 1 pt |
| 🔵 Cyan circle | Circle | 1 pt |
| 🟢 Green frog | Frog | 1 pt |
| ⭐ Rare gold circle | Circle | 5 pts + speed boost + 5 extra seconds! |

Your **high score** is tracked for the session.

---

## Special: Rare Pixel Pal

The rare **gold circle** (5 points) gives you a temporary speed boost and adds 5 seconds to the timer when collected. Keep an eye out for it!

---

## Map Features

The game map includes obstacles that block movement:
- 🌊 **Pond** — can't walk through it
- 🌲 **Trees** — solid obstacles
- 🏠 **Houses** — solid obstacles
- **Dirt paths** cross the map horizontally and vertically

Pixel pals always spawn in valid open positions (never inside obstacles).

---

## Title Screen

The animated title screen features:
- Bouncing **PIXEL PALS** letters in pink and green
- Scrolling ground with trees and a house
- Floating pixel clouds drifting across the sky
- Flying pixel birds that appear every 3 seconds
