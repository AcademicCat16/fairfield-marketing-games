# 📱 Social Sprint — Grow Your Account

A fast-paced clicker game that simulates growing a social media account. Post content, build your streak, and spend engagement on ad boosts to hit your follower goal before time runs out!

## Goal

Reach the **follower goal** before the **timer hits 0**.

| Difficulty | Time | Follower Goal |
|---|---|---|
| Easy | 75 seconds | 220 followers |
| Normal | 60 seconds | 250 followers |
| Hard | 55 seconds | 300 followers |

## How to Play

- Click **Post** (or press **Space**) to gain followers and engagement
- Post quickly to build a **streak** — higher streaks mean bigger follower gains
- Spend **30 engagement** on **Boost** (or press **B**) to activate a 6-second multiplier
- Post as fast as possible during the boost window for maximum growth
- Press **R** to restart at any time

## Scoring & Mechanics

**Streak multiplier** — each consecutive post increases your streak. Every 3 streak levels adds a bonus to follower and engagement gains. Waiting too long causes your streak to decay.

**Boost multiplier** — activating a boost multiplies your follower gains by 2.2x and engagement gains by 1.6x for 6 seconds.

**Random events** — every second there's a chance of a market event firing:

| Event Type | Effect |
|---|---|
| Trending audio, viral collab, peak posting time | +followers, +engagement |
| Algorithm change, content fatigue, bad hashtags | -followers, -engagement, streak cut in half |
| Audience insights | Small random bonus |

## End Screen

When the game ends (win or lose) you'll see:
- Final follower count
- Total posts made
- Best streak reached
- Number of boosts used

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| **Space** | Post |
| **B** | Boost |
| **R** | Restart |
