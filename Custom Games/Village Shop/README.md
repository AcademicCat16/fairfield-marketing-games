# 🏘️ Mini Trade Game — Village Shop

> Gather resources, craft goods, and sell them for gold!

---

## 🪵 About

Village Shop is a browser-based idle trading game. Gather wood and herbs from the wild, craft them into planks and potions, then sell your goods for gold. Simple, satisfying, and endlessly repeatable.

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. Click **Gather Wood** or **Gather Herbs** to collect raw materials
2. Once you have enough materials, click a **Craft** button to make goods
3. Sell your crafted goods with the **Sell** buttons to earn gold
4. Repeat to keep growing your gold total!

---

## ⚙️ Game Details

### Gathering
| Action | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Gather Wood | +1 Wood |
| Gather Herbs | +1 Herb |

### Crafting
| Recipe | Cost | Result |
|--------|------|--------|
| Craft Potion | 2 Herbs | +1 Potion |
| Craft Plank | 2 Wood | +1 Plank |

### Selling
| Action | Reward |
|--------|--------|
| Sell Potion | +15 Gold |
| Sell Plank | +10 Gold |

- Starting gold: **50**
- Potions are worth **50% more** than planks — prioritise herbs for maximum profit

## 🖥️ How to Run

1. Place all files in the same folder
2. Open `index.html` in any modern browser
3. No installation or internet connection required

---

## 💡 Tips

- Herbs → Potions is the most profitable chain: 2 herbs craft 1 potion worth 15 gold
- Wood → Planks earns 10 gold per 2 wood gathered — decent but herbs pay more
- There's no cap on inventory, so stockpile before selling for a big gold payout
